Cigar Etiquette

Ash your cigar properly by rolling it on the ashtray until the ash falls off.

Never share a cigar.

Let the cigar go out natrually. Never smash the cigar into the ashtray like a savage. It creates a foul smell. Let your cigar die a dignified death.

Do not lick your cigar.

If someone gives you a cigar, smoke the whole thing or not at all.

Never light or cut another man's cigar.

Don't bring in outside cigars into a smoking lounge.

Never use a cutter on a cigar that already has had someone's mouth on it.

Do not spit while smoking.

Do not dip your cigar in whiskey. Such actions are insulting to the cigar maker.

Do not smell a cigar unless you have purchased it.
