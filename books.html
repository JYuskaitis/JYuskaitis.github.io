Nat Sherman's Passion for Cigars by Joel Sherman

Nat Sherman was one of the most renowned cigar makers and shop owners in the world. This book provides his insight into the industry.

The Connoisseur's Book of the Cigar by Zino Davidoff

Zino Davidoff is responsible for one of the most luxurious cigar brands in the world knows as Davidoff. Mr. Davidoff was a true gentleman of the cigar world and such is exemplified in his book.

The Tobacconist Handbook: An Essential Guide to Cigars & Pipes by Jorge Armenteros

Mr. Armenteros is responsible for the premier tobacco education and certification company in the United States. This book is essential reading for anyone seriously persuing knowledge on tobacco and cigars.

The The Cuban Cigar Handbook: The Discerning Aficionado's Guide to the Best Cuban Cigars in the World by Matteo Speranza

The Cuban Cigar handbook is a great resource for familiarity with Cuban cigars, especially considering that physical encounters with Cuban cigars are difficult in the United States.

The Gentle Art of Smoking by Alfred H. Dunhill

Alfred Dunhill was one of the most famous cigar shop owners in the world. He was a master curator of luxury goods. His philosophy of cigars is seen in his book.
